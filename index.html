How to wander
◐Left thumb — walk & strafe (a joystick appears under your thumb).
↔Drag the right side — look around.
↥Jump — hop over low walls & ruins.
👁View — switch first / third person.
🌐World — moonlit hills ⇄ infinite plane.
✦Wonders — just walk close to a bell, a shrine, a tree; it responds on its own.
☾Rest — sit and be still; a haiku, and the sky drifts. Move to rise.
▶Demo — let the traveler walk the line for you.
📷Photo — hide the UI and capture the view.
